Welcome to BirdList PA

BirdList PA is a self-guided bird scavenger hunt designed for beginners.

This list includes some of the most common birds found in our area. Check off birds when you see them, there's no right order and no rush.

With a little time and attention, many people can spot most, or even all, of the birds on this list during a walk through a park or wooded area. Can you find them all?

A printable checklist is available on the Assets page.
