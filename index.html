The Problems I Solve

Many teams I work with feel:

  • Stuck in a development rut — it’s taken longer than expected, and there’s no clear end in sight
  • Uncertain about what to build — they have ideas, but no shared vision or process to move forward
  • Concerned about cost and complexity — the solution feels too big, expensive, or slow to build

If that sounds familiar, you're not alone — and there’s a better way.

My Approach

Clarify What to Build

It starts with a discovery process to align on vision and reduce guesswork:

  • Understand current status, goals, and roadblocks
  • Confirm problem fit, solution fit, and product/market fit
  • Use research, interviews, and analysis to identify where to focus

This creates a shared foundation to move forward confidently.

Answer Three Essential Questions

  • Is it feasible? — Can we realistically build this?
  • Is it viable? — Will it generate real business value?
  • Is it usable and valuable to users? — Does it solve a real problem in a usable way?

Build the Right Way

Once we know what to build, I help teams:

  • Define a clear, realistic vision and roadmap
  • Work iteratively — test, measure, adapt
  • Reduce waste, build in small increments
  • Leverage AI where it improves workflows or reduces friction
  • Stay aligned across product, design, and engineering
